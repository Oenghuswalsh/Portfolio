My Development Process

1.

Discovery

During this phase, I will work with clients to determine the project scope, objectives, and requirements. This includes gathering information about the target audience and their needs.

2.

Design

In this phase, I will create wire frames and design mock-ups to visualize your website’s layout and user interface. You will have the opportunity to provide feedback and make revisions.

3.

Development

During the development phase, the website’s features and functionality are created using coding languages and web development frameworks. The website will be tested for usability and compatibility.

4.

Deliver

Once your website is ready, it will be launched and made available to the public. I will ensure that your website is optimized for search engines and that all necessary analytics and tracking tools are in place.

5.

Depend

After launch, I will provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure your website remains functional and up-to-date. This includes fixing bugs, updating content, providing security measures, and providing you with technical support.
